gtfs_tripify¶

Most major transit municipalities in the United States publish realtime train (and bus) arrival information using a specification known as GTFS-RT. gtfs_tripify is a Python package for turning an archived stream of GTFS-RT messages into a historical record of train (or bus) arrival and departure times—information that was previously only available in schedule form.
